Previous experience in Tennis

My background has been as club coach for the past seven years. Additionally volunteering another two years as a high school coach prior to entering the tennis industry. Having worked with all ages of students in the developmental and competitive fields of tennis I was able to also have three years of experience teaching and coaching in the Tennis Canada Little Aces Program. In addition to coaching many athletes in junior and adult programs, I also was able to finish my degree from Douglas College in Coaching and Physical Education.
